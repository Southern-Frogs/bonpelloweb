Alpha Program Rules and Guidelines

  • App Invitation: You will receive an invitation to download the app from the Play Store. To participate, you'll need an Android device and a Gmail account.
  • App Updates: Whenever there's a new version of the app, you will find it in the list of apps to be updated in the Play Store. You can manually update it, or if your phone configuration allows, it will be automatically updated.
  • Bug Reporting: If you find a bug, please send a description to hello@bonpello.com. It would be great if you could include a series of steps to reproduce the bug and screenshots if they apply. Here are a couple of examples of bugs to give you an idea:
    1. - An ingredient breaks the ingredient recognition. For example, an ingredient with a symbol as a point like "C.I." used to create problems.
    2. - The app doesn't look good on your device, and the words are out of their containers.
  • Be Constructive: When providing feedback, please be constructive. Remember, I have feelings too! Your input helps make the app better, so let's keep the communication positive and helpful.
  • Rewards for Top Contributors: As a token of appreciation, we might give out gifts related to the app to the people who help the most. Your valuable contributions deserve recognition!
  • Known Issues: You can find a list of already identified issues at www.bonpello.com/alpha/known-issues. Check this page to see if your bug has already been reported or if there's a workaround available.
  • No Pressure: If you don't have much time to invest, no worries! You can still use the app as you normally would. Your usage and experience are valuable to us too.
